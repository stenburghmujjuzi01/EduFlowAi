Help Center

      
How do I start a course?
Use AI Chat or WhatsApp to tell EduFlow Ai what you'd like to learn.

      
How do certificates work?
Pass your final assessment (6/10 or higher) to receive a downloadable, verifiable PDF certificate.

      
Can I use this on WhatsApp too?
Yes - your account and progress are shared between WhatsApp and the website.
